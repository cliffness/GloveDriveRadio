GlobeDrive Radio (Phone Mode)

This is a placeholder WebView page.

Replace everything in app/src/main/assets/webapp/ with your 3D globe web app files.

From your JavaScript, call:

  • AndroidBridge.saveAllStations(JSON.stringify(stations))
  • AndroidBridge.saveFavorites(JSON.stringify(favorites))
  • AndroidBridge.saveRecents(JSON.stringify(recents))

Then open Android Auto: you should see GlobeDrive Radio under music apps.
